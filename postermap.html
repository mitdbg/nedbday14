The NEDBDay Poster Session will be held on the 4th floor of MIT building 32 (same building where the talks are happening), in the reception / faculty dining area outside the R&D pub.

To get to the 4th floor via the elevators, turn left when exiting the main lecture hall (32-123) and take the elevators to the 4th floor. Turn left out of the elevators, and then go through the swinging doors on your right. Walk straight ahead until you see the chain curtains around the reception area. See the maps below.

You can also take the stairs to the left of the main lecture hall up. At the top of the stairs, turn left along the walkway until you see another set of stairs to your left. Take these stairs up and you will see the reception area on your left.
